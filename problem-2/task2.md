Question 2: Uber Driver Performance Analysis
Uber Bangladesh is evaluating the daily performance of three drivers—Anowar,
Abdullah, and Jubayer. All three drivers started work at 8:00 AM and completed rides
throughout the day.

Anowar completed 18 trips earning 420 BDT per trip. He received a 750 BDT bonus.
Uber deducted 22% commission from his trip earnings. He spent 2,850 BDT on fuel,
350 BDT on tolls, and 450 BDT on parking. He worked for 9 hours and 15 minutes.

Abdullah completed 21 trips earning 390 BDT per trip. He received a 500 BDT bonus.
Uber deducted 22% commission. He spent 3,150 BDT on fuel, 500 BDT on tolls, and
300 BDT on parking. He worked for 10 hours.

Jubayer completed 16 trips earning 510 BDT per trip. He received a 900 BDT bonus.
Uber deducted 22% commission. He spent 2,600 BDT on fuel, 250 BDT on tolls, and
550 BDT on parking. He worked for 8 hours and 30 minutes.

Your Task
1. Calculate total trip earnings.
2. Calculate Uber commission.
3. Calculate final earnings after bonus and commission.
4. Calculate total expenses.
5. Calculate net profit.
6. Find the highest net profit.
7. Rank the drivers (1st, 2nd, 3rd) by net profit.
8. Identify the longest working duration.
9. Print Excellent Performance if net profit ≥ 6000, Good Performance if between 4000
and 5999, otherwise Needs Improvement.
Programming Rules
• Use only single-value variables.
• Use arithmetic operators, comparison operators, and if-elif-else.
• Loops, Lists, Tuples, Dictionaries, Sets, and Functions are not allowed.
